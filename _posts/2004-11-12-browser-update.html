--- title: Browser Update created: 1100266020 layout: blog --- There is a great article up on ArsTechnica about Malware for beginners. Basically it is software that does stuff to your computer that you don't want. If your computer is running slow or is doing things you don't want it to, check out this site. The most common ways to get it is to download P2P software such as Kazaa, Napster, iMesh or to use Internet Explorer (The big blue "E" on your desktop) or to install free stuff like smiley packs or screensavers.
