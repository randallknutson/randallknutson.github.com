--- title: Lunch Duty created: 1093625880 layout: blog --- Today I had lunch duty with the lower elementary which consists of Kindergarten, First and Second grade. I was a little hungry and my lunch hadn't arrived yet so I grabbed a donut from the school store. I was eating it as all the kids showed up. I was then lectured by quite a few of them that eating a donut for lunch was not healthy. I didn't feel like arguing with them. Later in the lunch one of the students, thinking he was clever, started calling me Mr. La-Dee-Da-Dee-Da. Oh the pain. Make it stop.
